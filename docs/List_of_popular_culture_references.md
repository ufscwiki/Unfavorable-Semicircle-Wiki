# List of popular culture references
While the main UFSC corpus is over-whelmingly cryptic; abstract; and even hostile, investigation over the years has uncovered multiple hidden-references to much-more main-stream media. Whether these are accidental or intentional; meant to be found; or have any artistic meaning or intention is unknown.

References by date of discovery:
* In 2017 the music in [RETIO](RETIO) was identified as Away Down East In Maine
* In 2020 the song Home-sick was found in [DELOCK](DELOCK); and again in [REDLOC](REDLOC) in 2024
* [In 2022 a tweet from @unfavorablesem](June 2022 twitter posts) linked the song The Most Beautiful Day, which is also-present in [SQEN](SQEN).
* In 2024 [audio composite](audio_composites) icons were matched to the video-games [Hearthstone](Hearthstone) and Doom 3
* In 2025 a theme-song from anime Fairy Tail was found in [LOYAL](LOYAL)
